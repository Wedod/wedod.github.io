CHAMPIONS LEAGUE 2025-2026
PREDICTION

A machine learning project to estimate club win probabilities and project top scorers, assist leaders, and player of the season for the 2025–26 Champions League.

Method (summary)

Collect — gather historical UCL matches, domestic league stats, and squad lists.
Engineer — build features for teams and players, normalize names, handle missing data.
Train — fit ML models, cross-validate, measure scoring.
Simulate — Monte Carlo simulations to aggregate match probabilities and player stats.
